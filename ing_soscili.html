Thai Chili Sauce

Thai Chili Sauce is almost similar to your typical chili sauce
except for the fact that it is sweeter because of the addition of sweetening ingredient
such as fruit or a refined sugar or honey.



Maepranom Thai Chili Sauce


This legendary Thai chili sauce from one of the famous brand in Thailand is perfect for dipping of almost anything.
Spring rolls, grilled chicken, fried fish cakes - just name it.
It almost impossible for it to not taste good with anything!




Life Thai Chili Sauce

Life Sauce products including Life Thai Chili Sauce
have became well known in Malaysia as the official sauce for
KFC fried chicken. With that being said, it is best for you
to have this sauce with your favourite fired chicken!




Kimball Thai Chili Sauce


Was introduced back in 1999 to Malaysian market,
this sauce has no artificial colouring, no artificial flavouring,
and no preservatives. If you want to have a hint of sweet and
spicy in your dish, this is definitely a great choice!
