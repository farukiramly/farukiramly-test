Chinese Cuisine

Chinese cuisine consist of Malaysian Chinese cooking which use local herbs inside their dishes. Its different among others Chinese Cuisine in the world. Chinese Cuisine in Malaysia has their own unique taste.

Chinese cuisine

Fried sauce noodle


Ingredients:
4 servings fresh or dried noodles
Sauce:
4 tbsp dry yellow soybean paste
1 tbsp sweet bean sauce, or hoisin sauce
300 ml water, 1 1/4 cup
2 tbsp cooking oil
1 star anise
2 stalk scallions, chopped
1 tsp ginger, minced
200 g pork, diced, 7oz (see note 1)
1 tsp Shaoxing rice wine

Vegetables:
150 g beansprouts
150 g fresh green soybeans/edamame
1 large cucumber, chopped
6 radish, sliced

How to:
1. In a bowl, dilute dry yellow soybean paste and sweet bean sauce
2. Saute' star anise, spring onion (the green part) and ginger.Stir in pork and rice wine.
3. Pour in the diluted sauce for 20 minutes.
4. Blanch beansprouts and green soybeans in boiling water. Take them out then drain.
5. In the same pot, cook noodles. Drain and rinse under cold water for a few seconds.

Naan Bread


Bread:
1/4 cup warm water
2 ts granulated sugar
1 1/2 ts active dry yeast, or instant (rapid rise)
3/4 cup warm milk
3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt, or natural plain yogurt
1/4 cup vegetable oil, plus 2 tablespoons extra for cooking
4 cups plain flour plus extra for dusting -- (19 1/2 oz. or 560g)
1 ts baking powder
1 ts salt

How to:
1. Mix water, sugar and yeast for 6 minutes.
2. Add milk, yogurt, oil, flour baking powder and salt
3. Knead the dough for 5 minutes
4. Rest the dough.
5. Roll the dough according to liking.
6. Place one piece of the naan on the oiled hot skillet and cook until bubbles form on top, about 1-2 minutes
7. Flip the nan until cook.
