Soy Sauce

Soy sauce, also known as Kicap here in Malaysia, is an East Asian liquid condiment of Chinese origin,
with a fermented paste of soybeans as the main ingredient.



Kicap Kipas Udang


Perhaps one of the most iconic soy sauce in Malaysia,
these soy sauce from the brand that has the image of prawn
in a fan shape, are our personal favourite to be used
in soy-based dish such as Ayam Kicap.
The strong traditional taste of soy bean will
definitely help to add the 'kick' to your dish.






Kicap Jalen


This local brand has been the ultimate choice
for Malaysian for years.
There are four variations available:
Manis, Masin, Lemak, Lemak Manis.
Each variation is invented for different taste bud of Malaysians.
You will definitely find one that suits you!





Kicap ABC


This Indonesian brand sweet soy sauce
is very thick and has rich caramel-colored sauce.
It is naturally sweetened with sun-dried palm tree sugar
and adds an aromatic, sweet flavor to any dish.
We personally love to pair this soy sauce with the spicy Ayam Gepuk!
