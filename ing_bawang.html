Bawang Goreng

Bawang Goreng is crispy fried shallots condiment, a popular garnishing to be sprinkled upon various dishes.
Even a plain congee can become tastier when you add a sprinkle of bawang goreng on top of it.
That's just how magical bawang goreng is!



Bawang Goreng Nuha


This premium bawang goreng brand offers you
crispy bawang goreng with healthier benefit
as it does not have any added sugar.
With only 3 ingredients: shallots, oil and salt,
this bawang goreng is a go-to bawang goreng
for your healthy diet choice!




Bawang Goreng Suci


This one is especially for those with big families.
A bigger package with cheaper price for longer use.
It's definitely a bargain for you without compromising the taste!




Bawang Goreng Sahimi


In contrast to Suci, Sahimi provides
various sizes of bawang goreng starting from 80g up until 1kg.
With this being said, anyone either a student
who want to buy it for personal use
or a hawker who want to use it for commercial use
can opt for this brand.
